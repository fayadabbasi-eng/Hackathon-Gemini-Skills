# Cape Town Summit Hackathon: Ideation & Strategy

This is the central hub for our Hackathon ideation, strategy, and script development.

## 🚀 Hackathon Workflow

Our workflow follows a "Survival of the Fittest" approach to move from raw ideas to a hardened pitch:

1.  **Diverge & Converge (`/div-conv`):** Generate multiple ideas, stress-test them through different "lenses", and converge on a "Winner" which is documented in `specs/spec-v1.md`.
2.  **Stress Test (`/grill-me`):** Relentlessly interview the team (or the agent) on the plan to find "Fatal Flaws" and refine the strategy.
3.  **Script Generation (`/script-gen`):** Convert the finalized spec into a high-impact, 2-minute pitch script with visual cues and specific "vibes".

## 🛠 Commands

-   `/div-conv`: Leads the team through a Double Diamond workflow to produce a hardened Markdown spec.
-   `/grill-me`: Relentless questioning to reach shared understanding and resolve decision branches.
-   `/script-gen`: Generates a modular 4-scene script based on a finalized idea or spec.

## 📂 Directory Guide

-   `.gemini/commands/`: Custom command definitions for the Hackathon workflow.
-   `specs/`: Storage for generated project specs and "Winner" documentation.
-   `scripts/`: Storage for generated pitch scripts and presentation materials.

## 🎯 Foundational Mandates

-   **SPEED-FIRST:** Focus on "Good Enough" versions that can be shipped or pitched quickly.
-   **SURVIVAL-DRIVEN:** Every idea must survive the "Interactive Grill" before being finalized.
-   **SPEC-NATIVE:** All builder instructions should be captured in a surgical, step-by-step Markdown format.
